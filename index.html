Who am I

My name is Sara and live in Gothenburg. I'm currently studying Front End Developer at Medieinstitutet which I started in the fall of 2022. I'm excited to see what the future holds, and I'm thrilled to deepen my knowledge within this rapidly evolving industry and become a part of the it-business.
